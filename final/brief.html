Comparison Project 1

First project for comparison is FlyPlatoon. This website successfuly delivers its product. The main interaction with the website is that it reacts on scroll. A user is able to scroll the page and receive different information about the rpoduct provided by the company. They show their planes applying different lighing sources and angles. The surface of the planes is very shiny. I'm guessing they use 3D graphics to display the model of the airplane. Sometimes there is just high quality photographs with bright and sharp color. Looks very similar to what we are building in my Computer Graphics class. The experience using the FlyPlatoon website is positive and clearly tells me about the services offered by the company.
